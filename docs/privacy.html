Privacy Policy

Car Show Entry Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter (such as show name, category, and entry date) is stored only in the app's local storage on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data, and the app makes no network calls to any server we operate.

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework; we never see your payment details.
